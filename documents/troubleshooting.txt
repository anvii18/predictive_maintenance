TROUBLESHOOTING QUICK REFERENCE GUIDE
======================================

EMERGENCY PROCEDURES:
If any machine reaches DANGER level (health score below 40%):
1. Alert supervisor immediately
2. Prepare for controlled shutdown
3. Do NOT restart without full inspection

─────────────────────────────────────

SYMPTOM: Temperature above 80°C (WARNING)
Priority: Medium

Step 1: Check coolant level in reservoir
Step 2: Inspect cooling fan — is it spinning?
Step 3: Clean any dust from cooling fins
Step 4: Reduce machine load by 20%
Step 5: Monitor for 15 minutes
Step 6: If temperature drops below 75°C — continue monitoring
Step 7: If temperature stays above 80°C — escalate to CRITICAL procedure

SYMPTOM: Temperature above 90°C (CRITICAL)
Priority: High

Step 1: Reduce machine load to minimum immediately
Step 2: Check coolant — top up if below minimum line
Step 3: Check cooling fan belt — replace if worn
Step 4: Schedule bearing inspection within 24 hours
Step 5: If temperature does not drop within 10 minutes — SHUT DOWN

─────────────────────────────────────

SYMPTOM: Vibration above 3.0 mm/s (WARNING)
Priority: Medium

Step 1: Check all mounting bolts with torque wrench
Step 2: Listen for unusual sounds (grinding = bearing failure)
Step 3: Check shaft alignment visually
Step 4: Add lubrication to bearing points
Step 5: Monitor for 30 minutes

SYMPTOM: Vibration above 4.0 mm/s (CRITICAL)
Priority: High

Step 1: Stop machine immediately for inspection
Step 2: Check bearing condition — look for heat discoloration
Step 3: Check shaft for visible damage or bending
Step 4: Replace bearings if any wear visible
Step 5: Do NOT restart until vibration cause identified

─────────────────────────────────────

SYMPTOM: Pressure below 3.0 bar (WARNING)
Priority: Medium

Step 1: Walk around machine checking for visible leaks
Step 2: Check inlet valve is fully open
Step 3: Bleed air from system using bleed valve
Step 4: Check filter — replace if clogged
Step 5: Monitor pressure for 15 minutes

SYMPTOM: Pressure below 2.5 bar (CRITICAL)
Priority: High

Step 1: Check all seals and gaskets for leakage
Step 2: Inspect pump impeller for wear or damage
Step 3: Pressure test all connections
Step 4: Replace seals if any leakage found
Step 5: If pressure cannot be restored — shut down and call senior technician
