MAINTENANCE HISTORY LOG

Date: 2024-01-15 | Machine: PUMP_A
Issue: High vibration detected (3.8 mm/s)
Root Cause: Main bearing wear due to insufficient lubrication
Action Taken: Replaced main bearings, realigned shaft, added lubrication
Parts Used: Bearing set SKF-6205, alignment shims
Result: Vibration reduced to 1.9 mm/s
Downtime: 4 hours
Technician: Prakash Kumar
Cost: ₹12,500

Date: 2024-01-28 | Machine: MOTOR_C
Issue: Temperature spike to 88°C during peak load

Parts Used: Industrial coolant 5L
Result: Temperature normalized to 68°C
Downtime: 2 hours
Technician: Priya Sharma
Cost: ₹3,200

Date: 2024-02-05 | Machine: COMPRESSOR_D
Issue: Pressure drop to 2.8 bar
Root Cause: Worn shaft seals causing air leakage
Action Taken: Replaced shaft seals, tightened all connections, pressure tested
Parts Used: Seal kit CS-204
Result: Pressure restored to 4.2 bar
Downtime: 3 hours
Technician: Amit Singh
Cost: ₹8,700

Date: 2024-02-10 | Machine: PUMP_B
Issue: Unusual noise during operation
Root Cause: Loose mounting bolts causing resonance vibration
Action Taken: Tightened all mounting bolts, added vibration dampeners
Parts Used: M16 bolts x8, rubber dampeners x4
Result: Noise eliminated, vibration reduced to 1.5 mm/s
Downtime: 1 hour
Technician: Rajesh Kumar
Cost: ₹1,800
